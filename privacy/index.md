# Privacy Policy

This is the privacy page for WinterWatch-Pro. This site is a simple project tracker and bookmark tool. This page describes the basic privacy practices:

- We do not collect personal data for visitors who simply browse the public site.

- The site may use standard web cookies for theme and session purposes depending on the hosting provider. No tracking or analytics scripts are installed by default.
